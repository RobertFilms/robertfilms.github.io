What is this game?

Run n' Jump is a simple game where you play as steven and you need to jump over obstacles.
The goal is to get as far as you can and complete objectives. The game will get faster as you progress. Good luck!


How to play?

Press the space bar to jump

Avoid the cacti and birds


Game features

The game will get faster

Birds move around

There are objectives to complete
